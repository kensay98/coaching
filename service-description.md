## Core Offer

**A confidential thinking partnership for founders and leaders who carry hard decisions.**
We go underneath the surface to untangle what is really going on, change the patterns that keep repeating, and get to decisions you can actually own. No agenda. No advice. Your answers, not mine.

---

## Who This Is For

Two tracks, same core work.

### Founders (5-100 people)
- You are holding decisions that nobody else can make.
- People dynamics keep costing you hires, momentum, or trust.
- You feel lonely at the top and cannot be fully honest with your team or investors.
- The company is scaling, but your own identity and leadership habits have not caught up.

### Ambitious Leaders
- You are stepping into bigger scope and feel the internal noise rising.
- Conflict, feedback, and stakeholder management are draining you.
- You overthink decisions and want a clean, grounded process.
- Imposter feelings show up right when visibility matters most.

### Founders
- **Make hard calls with clarity** instead of looping or delaying.
- **Change repeating people patterns** that are costing the business.
- **Have a place to be fully honest** without consequences.
- **Grow your leadership identity** as the company grows.

### Ambitious Leaders
- **Lead with clarity and confidence** in complex organizations.
- **Navigate conflict and feedback** without losing your center.
- **Own decisions** instead of outsourcing them or second-guessing.
- **Build presence and visibility** without performing a persona.

## What Changes

**Internal:**
- You feel calmer in uncertainty and more grounded in your choices.
- You hear the real signal beneath the noise.
- You stop repeating the same internal patterns.

**External:**
- Decisions happen faster and with less regret.
- Communication gets cleaner with teams and stakeholders.
- You show up differently in rooms that used to spike anxiety.

## Differentiation

- **Built-world context.** I am not coaching from the sidelines. I co-founded a startup, built products at a company acquired by Yandex, and led teams through fast growth.
- **Depth without abstraction.** I blend ICF coaching fundamentals, somatic work, Internal Family Systems, and meditation, but keep it practical and grounded.
- **No agenda, no advice.** The work is about your thinking, your decisions, and your ownership.
- **Confidentiality as a feature.** You can finally be honest about what you are carrying.

---

## How It Works

- **1:1 coaching sessions** — 60 minutes
- **Cadence** — usually weekly, adjusted to what is happening in your life
- **Engagement length** — typically 3-6 months
- **No curriculum** — you bring what is alive; we work on what matters now

---

## Not a Fit

- You are in crisis and need clinical support. Therapy is the right place for that.
- You want someone to tell you exactly what to do. That is mentorship or advising.
- You want quick fixes. The work takes time.

### Objection handling
- **"I just need advice."** Advice makes you dependent. Coaching helps you arrive at your own answer and own it.
- **"Is this therapy?"** No. Therapy is for "not okay" to "okay." This is for "okay" to "next level."
- **"Do you understand my world?"** I built in it. I know what it feels like to carry decisions alone.
