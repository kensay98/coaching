# Founder Clarity Reset (15 Minutes)

Use this whenever one decision keeps looping in your head.

## Step 1 — Name the decision (2 min)

Write one sentence:

- **Decision:**
- **Deadline:**
- **Why this matters now:**

## Step 2 — Separate facts from story (3 min)

Create two quick lists:

- **Facts I know:**
- **Stories I'm telling myself:**

## Step 3 — Run the 5 questions (5 min)

1. If fear was not deciding, what option feels most alive?
2. What option is most aligned with the company I actually want to build?
3. What am I optimizing for in the next 90 days?
4. What is reversible vs irreversible here?
5. What would make this decision "good enough" to move now?

## Step 4 — Define the smallest next move (3 min)

- **Next step I can take in 24 hours:**
- **Who needs to know:**
- **What success looks like by next week:**

## Step 5 — Pattern check (2 min)

Complete:

- The pattern that keeps repeating is...
- The trigger is...
- The new experiment for this week is...
